Site Name:Education

Why the name "Education"?

Becasue this website is designed to help others seeking to enhance their education or gain one.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide resources to other seeking to enhance their education or gain one

Scenarios

Scenario : 1

Lets say you have someone looking for free library resources because they cannot purchase a textbook for their university class. In one of my webpages I provide multiple links to different online libraries.

Scenario : 2

Say you were a grad student, whos struggling with time management and is looking for a set of resources on different time management strategies. In my first page I provide links to other sites and content that provide that.
